Covering Note

I’m driven by a genuine passion for building things from the ground up, whether that’s shaping visual identities, developing concepts, or crafting motion-led narratives. I’ve always been drawn to the process itself, taking an idea, exploring it, and gradually turning it into something tangible and considered.

Animation and motion design are a big part of that for me. They give me a way to tell stories that feel more engaging, human, and memorable. I enjoy thinking about pacing, rhythm, and how visuals evolve over time to guide someone through an idea, rather than just presenting a static outcome. There’s something really satisfying about bringing clarity and personality into a piece through movement.

I naturally gravitate towards projects where storytelling sits at the centre, translating concepts into clear, compelling visual journeys across different formats. I’m always looking to push that further through experimentation and refinement.
