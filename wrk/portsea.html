Throughout its history, Portsea Mews has been a home to craftsmanship in many forms. Originally built to stable horses and carriages, it later housed a Corn Store, reflecting its utilitarian beginnings. From 1954 to 2018, it became home to Handman & Collis, central London's oldest car repair garage. Today, Portsea Mews stands as a tribute to its crafted past, reimagined for modern living.
